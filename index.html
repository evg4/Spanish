Spanish

Spanish is a very useful language to learn.

Why?

It is spoken in many different countries including Spain, Mexico, Peru, Chile and Ecuador.

As Spanish is a romance language, once you master it, it will become much easier to learn other romance langauges like Italian, French, Portuguese and Romanian. That's because a lot of the vocabulary is the same and some of the grammar rules, too.


Here are some Spanish words to learn!

el perro = the dog

el gato = the cat

la madre = the mother

el padre = the father

los animales viven juntos con los humanos = the animals live together with the humans

¡Mira! ¡El perro está matando el gato! = Look! The dog is killing the cat!
